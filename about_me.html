France

Last summer, I worked as a student assistant for the Tufts in Talloires program. Though it was not a vacation, it was 7 weeks in France, so I feel like it counts. During that time, I fell in love with the outdoors; I hiked, I swam, and I ran, and it was the motivating factor for my half marathon in the fall. I learned a lot about French culture, and how good their chocolate is!

My Favorite Food


I love New England clam chowder! It's the best soup, and one of the best chowders I've had is at the Barking Crab in Boston. I like to throw in the oyster crackers at the beginning, letting them soften as I enjoy the rest of the bowl. Though I've never made clam chowder, I sure love to eat it!


My Favorite Book

The Way of Kings Brandon Sanderson In my opinion, Sanderson's best high fantasy novel. The story follows a young slave named Kaladin discovering new powers and struggling with the reality of this fantasy world.
